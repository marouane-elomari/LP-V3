I — The Broken System

Your agency is doing work
a machine should do.

Right now, somewhere in France, a letting agent is reading a payslip by hand. Calling an employer to confirm what is already on paper. Cross-referencing a tax return against an ID that may or may not be real. Then doing it again for the next file. And the next.

It takes 45 minutes per application. It has always taken 45 minutes. And it will keep taking 45 minutes — until something changes.

1 in 4 documents submitted to rental agencies has been manipulated. The fraud is getting smarter. The process is not.

This is not a workflow problem. It is not a software problem. It is a structural failure — and no CRM, no email tool, no spreadsheet is going to fix it.

II — The Historical Shift

This is not the first time
an industry automated its decisions.

In 1900, we automated physical labour. Factories ran on machines, not muscle.

In 2000, we automated information. Computers replaced filing cabinets and manual records.

In 2025, we automate decisions.

Every industry that still makes decisions by hand is about to change. The rental market is one of them — and it is long overdue.
